Inspiring future engineers through action.

"Who are we? Who are we?"

We are FIRST team 353! Every year we participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition, where we are given 6 weeks to design, prototype, build, and test a robot to compete at events with teams from across the world. Along the way, we impact our school and community with many different events to spread STEM everywhere!

Sponsors

Our club is supported through generous sponsors. They provide us funding in order to support our team and build amazing robots every year.

Leadership

Our team is ran by various students in our school ranging from 10th to 12th grade. We divide our work into a business team and a build team, with each one having their own subgroups. Check out our current board below!

Get involved

Making a robot and spreading STEM requires lots of effort from a lot of different people. We are always looking for more mentors, students, and other FIRST teams to reach out!
